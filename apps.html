NUMBERS

Logic game, you have 3 lives, you need to get rid of the blocks in the matrix when there are 2 blocks left, you need to click on any block, the round will end, and you get points. The rules are, there is a matrix of random numbers, a block can be removed by the sum or subtraction of the other two blocks. For example, to remove block 10, we can choose blocks 3 and 7, and then click on 10, this block will disappear. You can also do it with subtraction, to remove number 5, select numbers 7 and 2, the program itself will understand in what order to perform mathematical operations. But be careful, by choosing the wrong answer you will lose 1 life and the blocks will change their numbers.
